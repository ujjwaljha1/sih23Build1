This act provides social security to employees in case of sickness, maternity, disablement, or death due to employment-related injuries. Both employees and employers contribute to the Employees' State Insurance (ESI) scheme, which offers medical and cash benefits.

This act establishes the Employees' Provident Fund (EPF) scheme, which helps employees save money for retirement.Both employees and employers contribute a percentage of the employee's salary to the EPF account.

This act aims to ensure the safety, health, and welfare of dock workers employed in the Indian ports. - It covers various aspects of working conditions, safety equipment, and welfare measures for dockworkers.

This act regulates the conditions of work and safety in mines in India. It prescribes safety measures, ventilation, and welfare provisions for mine workers.

This act establishes a welfare fund for laborers employed in iron ore, manganese ore, and chrome ore mines.It is funded through a cess levied on the production of these minerals.
